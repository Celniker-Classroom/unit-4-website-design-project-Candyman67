Classical Genre

A classical genre is often described as a living map of human emotions, because it uses complexes arrangements to express feeling that words sometimes can’t be captured. Unlike modern songs that usually follow a predictable verse, a classical piece is designed to evolve, taking a simple melody and transforming it through different instruments and rhythms.
