Pop Genre

Pop music is built for instant catchiness. It uses simple “ Verses- Chorus” structures and repetitive “hooks” to make songs easy to remember and sing along to. While classical music explores long, complex stories, pop focuses on high energy.

Famous Pop Artist

Abba a legendary Swedish pop group formed in Stockholm in 1972 by Agnetha Fältskog, Bjorn Ulvaeus, Benny Andersson, and Anni- Frid Lyngstad. Which gained there international stardom after winning the 1974 Eurovision Song Contest with the Waterloo and dominated the global world after releasing the song Dancing Queen.

Jazz Genre

Jazz is all about making it up and swings. Unlike classical music, which usually follows a strict script, or pop, which sticks to a steady beat, jazz is unplanned. Musicians often make up their parts on the spot saving each other through their instruments.

Famous Jazz Artist

Miles Davis, the iconic American jazz trumpeter, was widely known for his restless innovation. He explored numerous developments in jazz, including jazz bands, modal jazz, and jazz-rock fusion, over a five-decade period.
