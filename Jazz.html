Jazz Genre

Jazz is all about making it up and swings. Unlike classical music, which usually follows a strict script, or pop, which sticks to a steady beat, jazz is unplanned. Musicians often make up their parts on the spot saving each other through their instruments.

Famous Jazz Artist

Miles Davis, the iconic American jazz trumpeter, was widely known for his restless innovation. He explored numerous developments in jazz, including jazz bands, modal jazz, and jazz-rock fusion, over a five-decade period.
